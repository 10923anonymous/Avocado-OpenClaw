## 🎮 Step-by-Step Building Instructions

### Phase 1: Starting Straight & CN Tower (Pieces 1-8)

**Piece 1: Starting Grid**
- Place: **Straight** (standard)
- Position: Ground level
- Notes: This is your start/finish line

**Piece 2-3: Build Speed**
- Place: **Straight** + **Straight**
- Position: Ground level, connect to Piece 1
- Notes: Gives players time to accelerate

**Piece 4-6: CN Tower Spiral (Elevated)**
- Place: **Ramp Up** → **Banked Turn 45°** → **Banked Turn 45°**
- Position: Rising elevation
- Rotation: Gradual left spiral
- Notes: Mimics the CN Tower's spiral structure

**Piece 7-8: Exit Spiral**
- Place: **Banked Turn 45°** → **Ramp Down (slight)**
- Position: Back to medium elevation
- Notes: Smooth transition to city grid

## 🎯 Key Design Elements Explained

### CN Tower Spiral
- **Why:** Iconic Toronto landmark
- **How:** Gradual banked turns rising up
- **Feel:** Spiral parking garage style

### City Grid
- **Why:** Toronto's famous grid street system
- **How:** Tight 90° turns like city blocks
- **Feel:** Driving downtown during rush hour

### Sideways Sections
- **Why:** Adds unique challenge
- **How:** 90° banked turns = driving on walls
- **Feel:** Defying gravity!

### Waterfront Jump
- **Why:** Lake Ontario is essential Toronto
- **How:** Long jump over gap
- **Feel:** Flying over the lake

### Underground PATH
- **Why:** Toronto's underground walkway system
- **How:** Covered/tunnel sections
- **Feel:** Subterranean racing
