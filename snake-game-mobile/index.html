Snake Game

🎮 How to Play

  • Move: Use Arrow keys, WASD, or Swipe on mobile
  • Eat: Collect eggs 🥚 for 1 point
  • Humans: Catch running humans 🏃 for 4 points
  • Avoid: Don't hit walls, other snakes, or yourself
  • 💩 Poop: After eating, your snake poops after 10 seconds
  • Eating Poop: Costs 3 points but makes you longer
  • AI Rivals: Competing snakes that also eat food
  • AI Obstacles: Snakes that just get in your way

🎨 Customization

Click the "🎨 Colors" button to customize snake, environment, and AI colors!
